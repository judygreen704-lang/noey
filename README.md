# Daily Reading Tracker

## Overview
The Daily Reading Tracker project is designed to help users track their daily reading habits. This project allows you to log the books you read, the time spent reading, and your thoughts or reflections about your daily reading sessions.

## How to Use
1. **Clone the Repository**: Start by cloning the repository to your local machine. Use the following command:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/judygreen704-lang/noey.git
   ```

2. **Navigate to the Project Directory**: Change into the project directory:
   ```bash
   cd noey
   ```

3. **Access the Tracker**: Open the tracker in your preferred method. This could be through a web interface if provided, or through a command-line interface.

4. **Log Your Reading**: Enter the details of your reading sessions:  
   - Title of the book  
   - Time spent reading  
   - Reflections or notes on the reading session

5. **Review Your Progress**: Frequently check your logs to see your reading habits and make adjustments as necessary to meet your reading goals.

6. **Contribute**: We welcome contributions! Feel free to submit issues or pull requests.

## Conclusion
Happy reading! Use this tracker to enhance your reading experience and keep track of your progress.
